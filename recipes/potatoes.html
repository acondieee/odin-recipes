Cheesy Potatoes

Description

Who doesn't love cheese and potatoes? This is a great, quick and easy meal that only requires 4 ingridents. Super tasty and goes great with just about anything!

Ingredients

  • 1 bag frozen diced potatoes
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 can of cream of chicken soup
  • 2 cups of cheddar jack cheese

Steps

  1. Put frozen potatoes in a slow cooker
  2. In a bowl combine the sour cream and cream of chicken soup and stir to combine. Pour mixture over potatoes
  3. Add cheese to the slow cooker and cook on high for four hours
  4. Enjoy!
