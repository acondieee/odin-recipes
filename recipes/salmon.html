Chili-Glazed Salmon

Description

I was never a big fan of salmon until trying this recipe. I'm a sucker for a good chili sauce and also recipes with only 3 ingredients! Easy meal prep meal or date night meal to impress that special someone!

Ingredients

  • 3 fillets of salmon
  • 1/2 cup chili sauce
  • 1/4 cup fresh scallions

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F
  2. Mix together salmon, scallion and chili sauce
  3. Bake salmon for 12-15 mins
  4. Enjoy!
